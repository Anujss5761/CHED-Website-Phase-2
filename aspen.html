Industrial Visit

On March 13 & March14, 2024 IIChE Student Chapter and Department of Chemical Engineering, MMMUT successfully organised an industrial visit at “Avadh Sugar And Energy Ltd. New India Sugar Mills Hata Kushinagar” under guidance of Dr. Shambhoo Sharan & Dr. Deepshikha Singh. Avadh Sugar and Energy ltd comes under KK Birla Group, a leading name in the sugar industry. The plant has a crushing capacity of about 7,000 tonnes of sugarcane per day and a Co-Generation Power Plant capacity of 35 MW Power. A total of 70 students form 2nd year of chemical engineering branch were visited at Industry.

The purpose of the industrial visit was to gain insights into various operations, processes, and technologies involved in the manufacturing of sugar. During the visit, the group was taken on a comprehensive tour of the industry floor, where they observed the manufacturing processes in action. From raw material processing to final product assembly, each stage of production was explained in detail by Senior Engineers. A highlight of the visit was learning about the company's rigorous quality control measures. The group gained an understanding of how the company ensures the quality and consistency of its products through various testing and inspection processes. The group learned about the role of advanced machinery in streamlining production processes and improving efficiency.
