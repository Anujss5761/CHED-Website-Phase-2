Best Project Poster Presentation Competition

The Department of Chemical Engineering and The IIChE Student Chapter organized a “Best Project Poster Presentation Competition” for final year students on May 11,2024 at Department of Chemical Engineering. The judge panel included esteemed invigilator, Dr. G.L. Devnani , Professor. There were 18 groups and a total of 89 students participated in the competition. The competition featured a diverse range of projects, each demonstrating student’s ingenuity and dedication to his/her respective areas of study. Projects were evaluated based on criteria such as innovation, scientific insights, and potential impact.
