ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

" Department of Chemical Engineering has been started in the academic session 2016-17 with intake of 60. First graduated in year 2020 and more than 50% student from first batch joined the industries, higher education at national and international level. Student received the fellowship of international agency like MEXT, Japan, etc. Department has well equipped laboratories such as Heat Transfer, Mechanical Operation, Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Mass Transfer Operation, Sugar & Alcohol Technology, Reaction Engineering, Process Dynamics & Instrumentation and Control and Modelling & Simulation. We are having infrastructure with modern facility for learning and research and strive to provide the excellent environment for teaching and learning. Regular guest lectures, seminar, workshop, and conference organized in collaboration with elite institutes like IITs, NITs, research institute and industries. Teaching can not be accompanist without the innovation and research. Department is in process of development of state of art facility for research in field of Chemical Products Processing, Modelling and Simulation, Biological systems engineering, Food Processing Technology, Green Energy, Industrial Biotechnology, Bioreactor Design and Electrochemical Energy Conversion. The major research facility available in Department are Spectrophotometer, COD analyser, TOC analyser. Research at Department is not limited to laboratories, strives to aimed at well-fare of deprived person leaving in society."
