Heat Transfer in Agitated Vessel

Agitated vessels are commonly used in industrial processes to mix and heat or cool liquids. Heat transfer occurs between the vessel contents and a heating/cooling medium through a surface area like a jacket surrounding the vessel or internal coils.

Heat Transfer Through Composite Wall

Heat transfer through a composite wall, made of two or more materials joined together, is an important concept in building science and various engineering applications. Because each material conducts heat differently, understanding this process is crucial.

Boiling Heat Transfer Unit

A Boiling Heat Transfer Unit is a piece of equipment designed to study how heat transfers during boiling. It typically consists of a vertical glass cylinder filled with a liquid and a heating element submerged inside. By controlling the power supplied to the heater, researchers can observe different boiling regimes.
